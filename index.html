The Ice House

A neighborhood
bar reborn from
the ground up.

Memorial Trail Ice House is a community-rooted craft bar on the edge of Houston's most beloved greenway corridor. Revived from a vacant lot, built with industrial bones, and designed for the people who actually live here.

From late-afternoon happy hours to Friday night energy — this is your spot.
